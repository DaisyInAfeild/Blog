Movies you MUST see !

Harry Potter:Sorcers Stone
Excellent Film!

The first (and the most crucial) movie in the Harry Potter Franchise is certainly one it's best- it happened to be my first exposure to the world of Harry Potter and the lifelong obsession that was about to follow. The first shot of Hogwarts- immediately mesmerizing with it's tall towers and windows glimmering with candle-light as the bewitching soundtrack by John Williams plays- is a testament to how well the film is made. Minor details like the gorgeous mountains and lakes that surround Hogwarts lurk in the background or at the edges of the frames and make the whole thing dreamier, and magical. It's all so enchanting that one doesn't nitpick the special effects .

Harry Potter:Chamber of Secrets
Excellent Film!

Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets is a successful sequel to the marvelous first film, with its own share of iconic moments and scenes. The movie takes us farther into Harry Potter's mind- the isolation he feels from the magical world for being introduced to it ten years late into his life- while taking us deeper into the history of Hogwarts and the Dark forces surrounding it. It also properly introduces us to the divisions and the discriminations that exist in the magical world- a commendable choice for what is essentially a movie for kids. The jungle, the castle and it's dark corridors, shapeshifting staircases and cloud kissing towers only get more intriguing and enchanting- imagine sleeping with the view of miles of mountains and lakes and the moon in the background. Many magical creatures make appearances- thankfully with far better special effects and CGI than its predecessor.
